# Privacy Policy for NKS – Neelkamal Stationers

NKS operates the NKS mobile application. This page informs app users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.

## Data Collection and Usage
We do not collect or store personal data except the information voluntarily provided by users (such as name, order details, or contact info).  
All data is securely managed using Google Firebase and Firestore.

## Third-Party Access
We do not share information with any third parties.

## Security
We value your trust in providing your information and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it.
