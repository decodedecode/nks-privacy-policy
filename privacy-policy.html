PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address, when you register or make a purchase.
  • Delivery address, which you provide so we can fulfil and deliver your orders.
  • Order history and NIKS Coins balance/redemption activity, stored as part of your account.

We also collect information automatically through third-party services we use to operate the app:

  • Device and crash data, via Firebase Crashlytics, to maintain the stability and security of our services.
  • Account and authentication data, via Google Sign-In, if you choose to log in that way.

2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We process your information to:

  • Create and manage your user account and authenticate your identity (via Google Sign-In).
  • Process payments securely (via Razorpay).
  • Deliver your orders to the address you provide.
  • Manage and apply NIKS Coins rewards and redemptions to your account.
  • Store and display product images and app media (via Cloudinary).
  • Monitor app performance and fix crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Communicate with you regarding your orders, account, or support requests.
  • Store your account, order, and address data securely (via Google Firestore).

3. WILL YOUR INFORMATION BE SHARED WITH ANYONE?

We only share information with the following third-party service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Google Firestore — used to securely store your account, order, and address data.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — used for crash reporting and performance monitoring.
  • Razorpay — used for processing secure online payments. We do not store your full payment card details ourselves.
  • Cloudinary — used to host and deliver product and app images.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

4. DO WE USE COOKIES OR OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES?

As a mobile application, we do not use browser cookies. We may use device identifiers and local app storage to keep you logged in and to remember your preferences within the app.

5. HOW DO WE HANDLE YOUR SOCIAL LOGINS?

If you choose to register or log in using a social account (such as Google Sign-In), we receive basic profile information from that provider, such as your name and email address, to create and verify your account.

6. IS YOUR INFORMATION TRANSFERRED INTERNATIONALLY?

Our servers and service providers may store data outside India in some cases (for example, Google Firebase and Cloudinary infrastructure). Where this happens, your information is protected under those providers' own data protection standards. Our business operations are based in India, and Indian law governs this policy.

7. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

We keep your information for as long as your account remains active, or as long as necessary to fulfil orders, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time (see Section 11).

8. DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM MINORS?

Our app may be used by individuals under the age of 18. If you are a minor using this app, we ask that you only do so with the knowledge and involvement of a parent or guardian, particularly when placing orders, sharing a delivery address, or making payments. By creating an account, you confirm that a parent or guardian is aware of and supervises your use of this app for ordering and payment purposes.

If we become aware that a minor has used the app without appropriate parental awareness, or if a parent/guardian contacts us with concerns about a minor's account, we will work with that parent or guardian to address the account, including deletion if requested.

9. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, in line with applicable Indian data protection law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. You can exercise these rights by contacting us using the details in Section 10, or by using the in-app account deletion option described in Section 11.

11. HOW CAN YOU DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT AND DATA?

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" option within the app, under Settings/Profile, or
  • Emailing us at neelkamalbooksandstationers@gmail.com with your registered phone number or email, requesting account deletion.

Once a deletion request is verified, we will delete your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required to retain certain records (such as transaction records) for legal or tax purposes.
